The Challenge

Women do the work.
The system doesn't pay them fairly.

In villages across Maharashtra and Karnataka, women wake before dawn to feed and milk their cattle. They carry the entire weight of dairy farming — yet the income rarely reaches them directly. The FDlite Foundation exists to change this system permanently.

⚖️

Unfair Pricing

Multiple middlemen cut into earnings before payments reach the farmer.

⏱️

Delayed Payments

Weeks-long waits for income make household planning nearly impossible.

🚫

No Financial Control

Payments flowing through others leave women with no say over their own earnings.

🔍

No Transparency

Farmers have no visibility into how their milk is priced or traded.

The Lakshmi Center Model

A community hub
that changes everything

Lakshmi Centers are village-level milk collection hubs that connect women farmers directly to the organized dairy supply chain — bypassing intermediaries entirely.

Your donation helps us open new centers, train women operators, and install solar-powered chilling equipment that keeps milk fresh from farm to consumer.

About the Foundation

The FDlite Foundation

The FDlite Foundation is the philanthropic arm of FDlite — India's women-owned dairy brand. While FDlite creates a market-driven supply chain, the Foundation raises funds to deepen impact: expanding Lakshmi Centers, training more women farmers, and building the infrastructure that makes financial independence possible in rural India.

We operate with radical transparency — every rupee of foundation funds is deployed directly to farmer programs, never to administrative overhead beyond what's legally required.

🏗️

Infrastructure

Solar-powered chilling units, collection equipment, and center setup costs

🎓

Training

Dairy management, business skills, and financial literacy for women farmers

🐄

Veterinary Care

Subsidised animal health services to improve yield and farmer income

📊

Financial Access

Banking, credit, and insurance linkages for women with no prior formal finance

🌱

Sustainability

Regenerative farming practices, feed management, and green operations

📣

Community Voice

Women-led governance of every Lakshmi Center, from operations to pricing decisions
